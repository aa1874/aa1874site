This is Chouverse.

A collaborative science fiction project (with a dash of fantasy) set in a multiverse of possibilities.

In this convoluted multiverse, some universes have more humans and near-human aliens than others, another has an totalitarian interstellar empire ruled by artificial general intelligence, and one looks like a fantasy world but with little-to-some modern-day gadgets. It spans the entire lifespan of universe(s). Conflicts might happen, even between universes...

Part soft science fiction, part hard, and part fantasy, the Chouverse (the chou being derived from the Japanese for "leap", "beyond", "super", "mega", ...) is a collaborative project hosted on the world's most popular development collaboration platform. And it's open for contribution...
